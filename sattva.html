Our Philosophy

"Where there is tea, there is hope."
— Arthur Wing Pinero

**Sattva** is more than a cafe; it's a feeling of purity, harmony, and balance. We meticulously prepare high-quality tea and pair it with the warmth of classic, comforting snacks, focusing on purity, harmony, and balance.

Our goal is to create a peaceful, beautifully designed space for you to reset. From the creamy texture of our **Bun Maska** to the bold flavor of our **Traditional Chai**, every item is crafted with intention and care. We are committed to sustainability, local partnerships, and providing a retreat from the everyday hustle.
